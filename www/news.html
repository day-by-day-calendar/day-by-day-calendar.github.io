July 4, 2014 - New Chrome (Chromium) extension for quick event/task adding right from your computer.

Specially for all Google calendar users we created a quick adding tool for events and tasks! Now you don't have to open Google Calendar page every time you want to create a task or an event, just click on Day by Day icon in browser toolbar and fill in all the necessary entries.

At the moment the extension is at the beta test stage. To install Day by Day extension, follow the link here.

To see the tasks and events added, click on Google Calendar link in the left low corner of the extension window.

December 13, 2013 - New color themes!

Many users asked to change the color themes of the app, they wanted the app to look strict and classy. The most active users even came up with the idea to implement Holo themes. As the suggestion grew very popular among other users, we are excited to inform that we are about to change the old themes to new Holo themes!

Unfortunately, there was no way to keep the old color themes and Holo themes at the same time, so Day by Day Team has to refuse from the old ones. But we understand that some users got used to the old standard color themes, that’s why we are ready to provide an apk for you if requested.

If you want to keep old standard color themes, please, turn off auto update and never install the app from Google Play again. If you update the app and get Holo themes but would love to keep the old color themes instead, please, let us know, we’ll help you with that info@appiens.com.
